About Us

White Cement Specialties is a locally operated, veteran-owned supplier proudly serving the Venice, Florida community and the greater Gulf Coast for over 40 years. We specialize in providing high-quality pavers, sealers, stucco materials, decorative concrete, and landscape supplies—available in both bulk and bagged options. As a trusted manufacturer of specialty concrete products, we offer everything from custom benches and stepping stones to masonry mixes and hardscape essentials. Known for our hands-on customer service, fast delivery, and competitive pricing, we are committed to supporting projects of all sizes—whether you're enhancing a backyard patio or managing a large-scale build. Our mission is to deliver reliable, personalized solutions with the professionalism and expertise our community has come to expect.

Driven by our vision to be the Gulf Coast’s most trusted partner in outdoor building solutions, we combine expert guidance, fast delivery, and competitive pricing to help bring your projects to life—whether you're enhancing a backyard or building a community.
