Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about products, delivery, and contractor accounts.

Who qualifies for a contractor account?

Contractor accounts are for verified Florida businesses. We verify your business registration through Florida Sunbiz and confirm your Florida EIN.

How long does it take to set up a cash contractor account?

For standard cash contractor accounts (no Net 30 terms), we can typically set it up the same day once verification is complete.

Is contractor pricing publicly available?

No. Contractor pricing is protected and reserved for verified contractor accounts. Public retail pricing is separate.

How do I receive contractor pricing?

After your account is verified, pricing and ordering details are handled by email through orders@whitecement.net.

Do you offer Net 30 terms?

Net 30 may be available for established accounts after a period of continued business and a successful credit evaluation.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

No—there are no minimums for orders.
