Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about products, delivery, and contractor accounts.

Do you sell pavers to homeowners?

Yes. Homeowners are welcome to walk in and purchase materials, and we can help estimate quantities for your project.

What brands of pavers do you carry?

We stock our exclusive pavers (best value) plus contractor favorites like Tri Circle, along with Tremron and Belgard selections.

Do you keep pavers in stock or are they special order?

We maintain a large in‑stock inventory—especially our exclusive pavers. Many other styles are stocked as well, and special orders are available when needed.

Can I return unused pavers?

All sales are final. Due to lot variations and the nature of hardscape materials, we do not accept returns or exchanges.

Do you recommend installers?

We can provide a list of locally recommended installers. We do not favor any one company—it's simply a helpful reference list.

Do you offer delivery for pavers?

Yes. Delivery options depend on material and location. For pavers, we deliver via flatbed with a Moffett forklift and can clamp‑deliver or deliver on pallets (pallet fees may apply).
