Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about products, delivery, and contractor accounts.

What types of delivery do you offer?

We deliver sand and aggregates by dump truck. For pavers and palletized materials, we use flatbeds with a Moffett forklift for offloading on site.

Do you deliver on pallets or can you clamp‑deliver pavers?

Most pavers can be delivered clamped (without pallets) or on pallets for an additional pallet fee—depending on the product and jobsite needs.

How fast can you deliver?

If the material is in stock, typical turnaround is 1–3 business days depending on current schedule and volume.

How is the delivery fee calculated?

Delivery fees are set by city and distance. There are no separate fuel surcharges—just the city-based delivery fee.

What areas do you deliver to?

Our primary delivery corridor is Tampa to Naples, and we can deliver statewide in Florida. Fees vary by city/distance.

Is there a minimum order for delivery?

No—there are no minimum order requirements.

Do I need to be on site for delivery?

We recommend someone is available by phone and that the delivery area is clear and accessible. For certain jobsites, on-site coordination may be required.
